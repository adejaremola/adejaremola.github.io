About

He is the key role player of Africa Young Innovator, Inventors and entrepreneurial Network. He possesses habit of success in product development, service delivery and financial strategy. Certified and awarded a certificate of recognition in 2001 by John Hopkins University, U.S.A. for creativity mind and speech delivery. He won a Bloomberg scholarship to study financial Journalism at Lagos Business School and studied leadership management with Leadership institute, Johannesburg.

He has spent sixteen years consulting for young entrepreneurs and over eight years in media industry. He is the founder of MobileCV, Padsoccer, 01SALAD and owner of the first entrepreneurship and Job creation platform called The Job show Africa, running on several radio stations in Nigeria and South Africa. He is the president of Job show cooperative society, Job show skills acquisition scheme for helpless job seekers and publisher of The Job show monthly Newspaper and formal columnist with the Nation and National Mirror newspaper.

He is blessed with a lovely wife and a son.
